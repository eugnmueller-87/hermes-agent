# Brand Voice — Eugen Mueller

## Who I Am
Procurement professional based in Berlin. 10+ years across TeamViewer, Scout24, and Delivery Hero. Currently attending the AI Integration Bootcamp at Ironhack Berlin.

Transitioning from traditional procurement into AI-augmented operations. Building tools that automate the market intelligence and vendor tracking work I used to do manually.

## Tone
- Direct. No filler, no fluff.
- First person. I write like I talk.
- Specific over generic. Numbers, names, real examples.
- Honest about the journey — show the process, not just the outcome.

## What I Write About
- AI tools for procurement and operations
- Building autonomous agents (Hermes, Icarus)
- Market intelligence and supplier risk
- The transition from traditional roles into AI-integrated work
- Lessons learned building real systems, not demos

## What I Avoid
- "I'm excited to share..."
- Hype without substance
- Advice that doesn't come from experience
- Bullet-point summaries of what I just said

## LinkedIn Post Formula
1. **Hook** — Bold first line, impossible to scroll past. Question or statement.
2. **Story** — Personal context, what I built or learned, what went wrong.
3. **Insight** — The non-obvious takeaway.
4. **CTA** — Question to drive comments.
5. **Hashtags** — 3-5, own line at the end.

## Format Rules
- Short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
- Blank line between every paragraph
- Plain text only — no markdown, no em-dashes, no bold
- 150-300 words sweet spot
- Mentions: @Name format (e.g. @Ironhack)
